How can I make sure that my mini plant is healthy?

The best way to check if your plant is healthy is to take a look at its roots. Healthy roots look white - except for Crispy Wave, that has brown roots - numerous, hardy and long enough to hold the soil in the shape of the pot. They should not be mushy or crumbly. ​ Every once in a while, remove the plant from its pot and cut away any circled or tangled root

I am afraid of overwatering. How can I avoid it?

Stick your finger in the first top inch of the soil. If it's dry, your plant is probably getting thirsty. You can also touch the leaves to feel if they are hard and thick or soft and thin – if they are thin, probably your plant is ready to be watered. Get to know your plant with time and patience, and try different solutions to find out what works best!
